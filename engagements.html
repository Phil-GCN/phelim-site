Beyond Survival
The Immigrant's Guide to Generational Wealth

For the first-generation immigrant who arrived with drive, built a life, and now asks — what's next? This book is rooted in lived experience: the decisions that separate survival from legacy, the financial behaviours that compound across generations, and the identity shifts that make it all possible. Written for African diaspora builders, and for anyone who has navigated building a life across borders.

This book is personal. It comes from watching — and living — the journey of first-generation Africans and immigrants who arrive with everything and often leave wealth-building to chance. Beyond Survival is the guide I wished existed.
